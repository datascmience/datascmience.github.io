Contact QR

Privacy & Security

Local Processing: Your data never leaves your device. The QR code is generated entirely within your browser; no data is sent to a server and no logging is performed.

How it Works

">

vCard Generation: This app formats your text into a vCard (.vcf) standard string. This is a globally recognized format that tells your phone's contact app exactly where the name, phone, and email belong.

Deterministic: QR generation is deterministic. This means the same input will always produce the exact same QR code image, regardless of when or where it is generated.
